## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to connect any accounts or integrations?**
A: No. CostPilot works from the information you enter manually — which tools you use, which plan you're on, and what you're paying. Nothing is pulled from your bank, your email, or your subscriptions automatically. You're in full control of what you share.

---

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
A: The estimates are based on publicly available plan pricing and a set of rules built around common inefficiency patterns — duplicate tool subscriptions, billing cycle mismatches, and plans with features teams at your stage don't yet need. They're directionally accurate, not guaranteed. Think of them as a starting point for a 30-minute internal conversation, not a precise invoice.

---

**Q: Who is CostPilot for?**
A: Primarily for technical co-founders, CTOs, and engineering leads at startups between 3 and 50 people — the person who either owns the AI tool budget or has strong influence over it. If your team has adopted 3 or more AI tools in the last 12 months and nobody has formally reviewed the stack, this tool is for you.

---

**Q: What happens to my audit data?**
A: Your audit is saved and assigned a unique URL so you can share it. The public version of that URL shows tools and savings numbers but strips any identifying information. We don't sell your data or use it to contact you unless you explicitly opt in.

---

**Q: Can I share my results with my team?**
A: Yes — that's the point. Every audit generates a unique shareable link you can drop into Slack, email to your co-founder, or present in a budget review. The shared page is designed to be read by someone who didn't fill out the form — clean, scannable, and focused on the numbers that matter.
